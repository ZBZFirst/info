Projects

Explorations of the Question "Why?"

These projects represent different pursuits of knowledge, curiosity, and application.

  • Project 1: The Infinite Query

    A deep dive into recursive questioning—an algorithm that keeps asking *why* until no further depth can be found.

  • Project 2: Data Mapping the Unanswered

    Visualizing the most common unanswered questions found in online forums and academic papers.

  • Project 3: The Random Insight Generator

    A system that randomly combines historical quotes, research abstracts, and philosophical ideas to spark creativity.

  • Project 4: The Why? Web Crawler

    A simple web scraper that finds the most frequently asked 'why' questions across the internet.

  • Project 5: Philosophy in 100 Lines

    Summarizing the core ideas of the world's greatest philosophies in exactly 100 lines of plain text.
