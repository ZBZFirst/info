Research Publications

Publishing research is fucking hilarious. It's just doing a science report like in highschool but with Adults. My favorite part was Author listing and how much drama came around it. There was always an argument for every paper on the author listing. You could tell tempers flared once everyone left. That's also why Author Listing discussions happened at the end for my group. People got mad about their positioning and would leave the group. It is also a reason why I don't do group work anymore.

I never received any payment for my research. All research was done on my own time off the company clock. As a result, many hours of Fatherhood were lost to these research endeavors. As a result, my honest opinions of them are below. Don't do research at Loma Linda University, you do it for free there.
